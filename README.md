##  Introduction
 This project was created for **Pizza Sales data to gain insights into Business Perfromance,** to solve common problem: Using a *real-world dataset of Jan/2015 - Dec/2015 Pizza Sales* this project used in *SQL and Power BI*.

 ##  Problem Statement
 ##  **KPI's Requirement**

- We need to analyze key indicates for our Pizza Sales data to gain insights into our business performance.

- Specially, we want to calculate the following metrics

## 1. Total Revenue:
  - The sum of the total price of all records.

![Query](/Images/Total_Revenue.png)

## 2. Average Order Value:
  - The average amount spent per order, calculated building the total revenue by the total number of orders.

![Query](/Images/Avg_order_value.png)

## 3. Total Pizza Sold:
  - The sum of the quantities of all pizzas sold.

![Query](/Images/Total_pizza_sold.png) 

## 4. Total Orders:
  - The total numbers of orders placed.

![Query](/Images/Total_orders.png) 

## 5. Avgerage Pizzas per Order:
 - The avg. no. of pizzas sold per order, calculated by dividing the total no. of pizzas sold by the total no. of orders.

![Query](/Images/Avg_pizza_per_order.png)

 ##  **CHART Requirement**
 - We would like to visualize various aspects of our pizza sales data to gain insights and understand key trends. We have identified the following requirements for creating charts.

 ## 1. Daily Trend for Total Orders
 - Create a bar chart that displays the daily trend of total orders over a specific time period. This chart will help us identify any patterns of fluctuations in order volues on a daily basis.

![CHART](/Images/Daily_trend_total_orders.png)

 ## 2. Monthly Trend for Total Orders
 - Create a line chart that illustrates the hourly trend of total orders throughout the day. This chart will allow us to identify peak hours or periods of high order activity.

![CHART](/Images/Monthly_trend_total_orders.png)

 ## 3. Percentage of Sales by Pizza Category
 - Create a pie chart that shows the distribution of sales across different pizza categories. This chart will provide insights into the popularity of various pizza category and  their contribution to overall sales.

![CHART](/Images/salesby_pizza_size.png)

 ## 4. Percentage of Sales by Pizza Size
- Generate a pie chart that represents the percentage of sales attributed to different pizza sales. This chart will help us understand customer preferences for pizza sizes and their impact on sales.

![CHART](/Images/sales_by_category.png)

 ## 5. Total Pizzas Sold by Pizza Category
- Create a funnel chart that presents the total no. of pizzas sold for each pizza category. This chart will allow us to compare the sales performance.

![CHART](/Images/Pizza_soldby_category.png)

 ## 6. Top 5 Best Sellers by Revenue Total Quality and Total Orders.
 - Create a bar chart highlighting the top-5 best-selling pizzas. Based on the revenue, total quality, total orders. This chart will help us identify the most popular pizza options.

![CHART](/Images/Top%205%20pizzas%20by%20revenue.png)

 ## 7. Bottom 5 Best Sellers by Revenue Total Quality and Total Orders.
- Create a bar chart showcasing the bottom- selling pizzas. Worst selling pizzas based on the revenue, total quantity, total orders. This chart will enable us to identify underperforming or less popular pizza option.
